About the Role

Noino Arus Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a fast-growing, innovative technology firm dedicated to delivering scalable software solutions to global enterprises. We are looking for highly motivated, passionate, and adaptable entry-level Software Developers to join our engineering team.

As a fresher, you will undergo an intensive technical onboarding program, collaborating with senior engineers on live products. You will write clean code, participate in system design, and contribute to the full software development lifecycle.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop, test, and deploy highly scalable and robust software applications.
  • Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams including UI/UX, QA, and Product Management.
  • Write maintainable code following industry best practices in your chosen tech stack.
  • Troubleshoot, debug, and optimize application performance.

Qualifications & Technical Skills

  • B.Tech/B.E., MCA, BCA, or M.Sc/B.Sc in Computer Science (2023-2025 graduates).
  • Strong foundation in Data Structures, Algorithms, and Object-Oriented Programming (OOP).
  • Hands-on academic or project experience in at least one: Python, Java, C++, C#, JavaScript, or Flutter.
  • Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills.

Interview Process

  1. Round 1: Online Aptitude + Core Coding Assessment
  2. Round 2: Technical Interview (System Design & Logic)
  3. Round 3: HR Interview (Culture Fit)
